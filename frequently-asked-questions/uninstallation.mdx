  <Accordion title="I Archived An Assessment, Why Is it Not Uninstalled?">
    When archiving an assessment, you will receive the following notification:

    ![Screenshot2025 04 22at11 39 04AM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-04-22at11.39.04AM.png)

    Upon seeing this, please go back and confirm that all devices are online, or they will not be uninstalled upon assessment archive. It is recommended that you first manually uninstall using the "uninstall all button" in red.

    ![Screenshot2025 04 22at11 40 43AM Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-04-22at11.40.43AM.png)
