  <Accordion title="Nodeware">
    Click Account > Integrations and turn on Nodeware. You will be prompted to enter the Nodeware API key and save.
    For any assessment, you can enable Nodeware by visiting Setup > Optional Steps > Connect Nodeware and adding the customer ID from Nodeware for the same company.
    Once done, it will begin fetching data. All data will appear under Security > Network in 24 hours.
